Oakland Mills High School Computer Science (Club) (OMCS / OMCSC)


The OMCSC is a club where people can work together towards a common goal using computer science. This year that goal is a game! This website provides information on the club, its members, and what's happening. Here's a link to our GitHub account.

Quick FAQs

  • When do you meet?
    We meet on Tuesdays from 2:15 to 3:15. If you can't make it at that time, don't let that discourage you from joining. You should try contacting us and ask about what you can do. Be sure to describe your situation.
  • Where do you meet?
    We meet in the Computer Science room, Room 404.
  • Who is your sponsoring teacher?
    Mr. Mosier is the Compuer Science Club's sponsor. He lets us use his room to meet, and will answer questions that we have as well.
  • I'm interested but don't know anything about Computer Science, what do I do?
    You should check out the About page. Having no prior knowledge won't hold you back, everyone is here to help.
  • What other things do you do?
    A few things. Besides our project, people are able to help each other learn new ideas and skills. Also, occasionally we have pizza or some other food during a meeting. Another thing is that if you have a request for a small project or other help, some people in this club might help you with it. All you'd have to do is either email us or show up at a meeting and ask.
The Oakland Mills High School Computer Science Club was created in the 2016-2017 school year.
