Oakland Mills High School Computer Science (Club) (OMCS / OMCSC)


The OMCS Club is where you can join to learn how to design and create software, websites, and other things. This website is built and managed by members of the club, and is used as an information source. We're happily signed up with HackClub, an organization which helps provide schools with resources to assist their computer science clubs. Follow that link to learn more about who they are and how they help.

Quick FAQs

  • When do you meet?
    We meet on Tuesdays from 2:15 to a now unspecified time, due to our recent pairing with HackClub.
  • Where do you meet?
    We meet in the Computer Science room, Room 404.
  • Who is your sponsoring teacher?
    Mr. Mosier is the Compuer Science Club's sponsor. He lets us use his room to meet, and will answer questions that we have as well.
  • I'm interested but don't know anything about Computer Science, what do I do?
    You should check out the About page. Having no prior knowledge won't hold you back, anyone can join. HackClub provides resources to help assist in learning.
  • What other things do you do?
    In addition to the HackClub activities provided, groups of people can choose to start their own group projects and work on them together. Using GitHub, people can easily create team projects.
  • Are you willing to work with other clubs?
    Yes, if clubs wish to work on something with us we're willing to discuss.
