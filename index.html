Oakland Mills High School Computer Science (Club) (OMCS / OMCSC)


The OMCSC is a club where people can work together towards a comman goal using computer science. This year that goal is a game! This website provides information on the club, its members, and what's happening.

The Oakland Mills High School Computer Science Club was created in the 2016-2017 school year.
